About Stephen Curry

Stephen Curry, born March 14, 1988, is a professional basketball player for the Golden State Warriors. Known for his exceptional shooting abilities, he is regarded as the greatest shooter in NBA history. Curry changed the game by popularizing the three-point shot and leading the Warriors to numerous championships.

Curry's rise to stardom was not typical. Despite being overlooked by major colleges due to his size, he shined in college basketball at Davidson. His success led to him being selected as the 7th overall pick in the 2009 NBA Draft. Over the years, Curry has earned multiple NBA championships, MVP awards, and set numerous records.

Achievements

  • 4× NBA Champion (2015, 2017, 2018, 2022)
  • 2× NBA MVP (2015, 2016 - Unanimous)
  • NBA Finals MVP (2022)
  • NBA All-Time Leader in Three-Point Shots Made
  • 9× NBA All-Star
  • NBA Scoring Champion (2016, 2021)
  • 2× Olympic Gold Medalist with Team USA
